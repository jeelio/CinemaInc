Miscellaneous Info

If you have general comments or concerns, drop us a line, give us a call, or shoot us an email.

If you wish to suggest a film for the board to consider, please provide the film's title, release year, and director.

If you wish to receive our monthly film notes, you must provide us with a working email address. You will also receive an email confirmation of your order. Don't worry, The Cinema, Inc. will not share your contact information with any other organization.

Season tickets are mailed to members in late August. This ticket is your pass to all twelve movies in the season. You will need to show it upon arrival for admittance to each movie. Please keep it in a safe place and do not lose it!
