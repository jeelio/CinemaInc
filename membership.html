Become a Member

Our season runs from September to August. Movies are screened the second Sunday of the month at the Rialto Theatre on Glenwood Avenue in Raleigh's Five Points neighborhood. Admittance is restricted to members.

If you are interested in membership, download the PDF brochure for our 2018-19 season and send in your form with check or money order*. Tickets are still $20 for twelve films. We cap membership based on seating capacity of the theater. The last few seasons have sold out, so don't wait! You will receive email notification of membership. Passes will be mailed to members in late August in time for the first movie of the season.

* The Cinema, Inc. accepts only checks or money orders as payment. We do not accept credit cards or PayPal.
